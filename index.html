Who is Sticko?

Sticko is the consequence of hate and anger.

Losing hope in the world, he had isolated himself from the rest. After

staying away for enough time, his darkness consumed him and then he changed,

directing his life esscence towards some digital programs. Now he is lost without knowing

how to redirect it and crying for the desolation of his existence.

Where did Sticko come from?

Born on October 13th in the dimness of night, he was handed over to this world from the pits of hell to rewrite his destiny.
